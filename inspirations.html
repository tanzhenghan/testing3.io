1) Tiger Woods

His achievements

Tiger Woods is my biggest inspiration in Golf. He is said to be one of the greatest, if not the greatest, golfer of all time. He has won 82 events on the PGA Tour (tied 1st) and of which, 15 were major championships (all-time 2nd highest).

His qualities

I admire his work ethic, confidence, and his ability to execute difficult shots and perform under tremendous pressure.

2) Mardan Mamat

His achievements

Another of my inspirations in golf is Mardan Mamat, Singapore's No. 1 golfer for more than 10 years. He was the 1st player from Singapore to win a European Tour event and to play in The Open Championship, one of the major championships in golf.

He came from humble beginnings, and he initially worked as a caddie in a golf club. He would hone his golf game when he had no caddie duties.

His qualities

He is said to be a down-to-earth sportsman, and I admire his work ethic, and his perseverance in performing well in a sport, that not many people play and succeed in Singapore.
