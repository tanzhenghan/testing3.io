Beginnings

Introduction to the Game

I was introduced to the game of golf by my dad. He taught me everything about the game, ranging from the rules to the techniques involved.

Every beginner would start off at the driving range, and that was what we did. We frequented the nearby Champions Golf Driving Range, and that was where he taught me the basics of the game.

My 1st Round of Golf

After numerous rounds of practices at the driving range, I played my 1st round of golf at Royal Tanglin Golf Course. It was suited for beginners because it was purely a Par 3 course (i.e. a short course).

Subsequent Rounds of Golf

As I slowly improved my playing abilities, my 2nd round of golf was played at Mandai Executive Golf Course, which was also suited for beginners because it was a relatively short course too.

Due to the high cost of playing in golf courses in Singapore, as a family, we travelled overseas to play. My 1st overseas golf game was played in Bintan Island, Indonesia . Unlike the previous beginner courses I played in Singapore, the golf course at Bintan Island was better maintained; which enabled me to play a better game. Also, as the golf course was near the sea, the view I had while playing was truly breathtaking, and it is one I remember up till this day.
